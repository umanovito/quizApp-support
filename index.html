Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Quiz Master.

How do I create an account?

You can create an account using your email, or sign in with your Google, Apple, or Facebook account. Choose the option that's most convenient for you during the sign-up process.

How does the scoring system work?

Your score is based on the number of correct answers, the difficulty level of the quiz, and how quickly you complete it. Maintaining a daily streak also gives you bonus points on the leaderboards.

How do I upgrade to Premium?

You can upgrade to Premium from the profile section. We offer various subscription options. All purchases are processed through Apple's App Store.

Can I restore my purchases?

Yes, if you've previously subscribed to Premium, you can restore your purchase. Go to the profile section, select "Restore Purchases," and follow the prompts.

How do I change my profile picture?

Go to your profile, tap on your current avatar, and select "Change Profile Picture." You can upload a new image from your device's gallery.

What happens if I miss a day in my streak?

If you miss a day, your streak will reset. However, all your accumulated points and achievements remain. You can start building a new streak the next day.
